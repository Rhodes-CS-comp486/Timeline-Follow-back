Timeline Followback Instructions for Alcohol Use

  • Log your alcohol use everyday for the past 3 months by clicking on a date on the calendar.
  • On the days you drank alcohol, select the box saying "Did you drink alcohol today?" and answer the question to the best of your ability.
  • On the days you didn't drink alcohol, ensure you still click the "Save Entry" button. Doing this shows that you did not drink alcohol that day as opposed to just not filling out that day yet.
